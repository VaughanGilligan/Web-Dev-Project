About me

My name is Vaughan Gilligan. I am a 20 year old photographer, among many other things, based in Co. Sligo, Ireland. My specialities are astrophotography, automotive, and nature.

Most of my photos are captured on an Apple iPhone 11 Pro. I like the Pro series iPhone because they have 3 12MP cameras; wide, ultrawide and telephoto

I believe that anyone is capable of capturing a stunning photograph. You don't need any expensive, specalist equipment nor do you need to be well versed in the likes of Adobe Photoshop. There are many free examples of photo editing software that can go toe-to-toe with Photoshop, such as Google Snapseed.
